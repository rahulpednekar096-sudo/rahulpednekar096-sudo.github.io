Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

RDroid Apps operates Android applications, Windows tools, and the website https://rdroidapps.com. This Privacy Policy explains how our apps, tools, and website handle user data and privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not intentionally collect or store personally identifiable information through our website or Windows tools.

Our website may use trusted third-party tools for analytics and account features, which may collect limited non-personal or aggregated information. For example, if you choose to create an account or sign in using Google authentication, Google may collect certain information as described in their privacy policy. Additionally, we may use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect aggregated data about website usage, such as the number of visitors, pages visited, and general geographic location. This data is used to improve our website and services and is not personally identifiable. We do not use this data for third-party advertising purposes on our website. Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information on how they handle data.

2. Website Data & Cookies

We currently do not display third-party advertisements on this website.

Our website (rdroidapps.com) may use cookies and similar technologies to operate core features, remember preferences where applicable, and measure site traffic through analytics. These technologies are not used to serve third-party advertising on this site.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking or deleting cookies may affect how certain parts of the site work.

3. Android Apps

Data practices for individual Android apps (including any optional analytics or features) are described in each app’s own privacy policy or in-app notices. Please review the policy for the app you use for details.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services in connection with our website and online services:

  • Google Firebase – authentication, app backend, and related features where account sign-in or cloud features are offered
  • Google Analytics (GA4) – aggregated website usage statistics to understand how visitors use rdroidapps.com

These services may collect anonymous or aggregated data according to their own privacy policies. You can learn more in Google’s privacy documentation and, where available, adjust cookie or analytics preferences through your browser or device settings.

5. Windows Tools & Local Processing

Our Windows tools are designed to work locally on your device. Files processed using these tools are not uploaded to our servers unless explicitly stated.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our apps, tools, and website are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user privacy and ensure that our tools and services operate securely. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.
