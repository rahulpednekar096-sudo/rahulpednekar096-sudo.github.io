Easy Music: Simple Note-Based Music Creation

Easy Music is a beginner-friendly music creation app designed for basic melody composition using individual musical notes. It allows users to experiment with note timing and simple sequencing in a clean, offline environment.

This app is intended for learning, experimentation, and casual music creation, focusing on simplicity rather than advanced or professional audio features.
