1. Overview

Buddhist Calendar is an informational and utility application for Buddhist calendar events, cultural content, meditation tools, reminders, and related features.

The App does not require user registration, login, or account creation. You can use most features without providing your name, email address, phone number, or other directly identifying personal information to us.

We designed the App to minimize data collection. Most user-created content stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically by us and by third-party services integrated into the App, including:

- App usage and interaction events (for example: screens viewed, buttons tapped, navigation paths, and feature usage)
- Anonymous analytics and performance data through Google Firebase Analytics
- Device and diagnostic information such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, and general performance data
- Advertising-related data through Google AdMob, which may include device identifiers, ad interaction data, and approximate location derived from IP address, in accordance with Google's policies
- Crash, stability, and technical logs that may be generated by Google services used in the App

This information is generally collected in aggregated or pseudonymous form and is not used by us to identify you personally.

2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores certain information locally on your device using on-device storage (for example: app database and app preferences). This information is used only to provide App functionality and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated otherwise in this policy.

Locally stored information may include:

- Alarms and reminders you create (title, time, repeat settings, sound choice, and related preferences)
- Daily Planner tasks, notes, and completion status
- Meditation session history (duration, completion status, and related settings)
- Favorite quotes or similar saved preferences
- App settings such as country selection, calendar year, audio volume, and display preferences
- References to audio files you choose from your device (see Section 2.3)
- Cached copies of calendar, event, chant, and other content downloaded for offline use
- Widget-related cached data displayed on your home screen

Because this data remains on your device, uninstalling the App or clearing app data will remove it.

2.3 User-Selected Content from Your Device

If you choose to import or select audio files from your phone or tablet (for example: for meditation sounds, chanting audio, or alarm tones), the App accesses only the files you explicitly select through the system file picker.

The App may store a local reference (such as a content URI) so the selected file can be played later. We do not upload your personal audio files, photos, contacts, or other device files to our servers.

2.4 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require or intentionally collect:

- Your name, email address, or phone number for normal App use
- Precise GPS or continuous location tracking
- Contact lists, SMS messages, call logs, or photo galleries (except the specific files you manually choose to share with the App through Android's file picker)
- Payment or financial information (the App is free to use; any future paid features would be handled by Google Play if added)
- Health or medical records

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- To operate, maintain, and improve the App and its features
- To understand which features are used and to improve user experience
- To monitor reliability, diagnose technical issues, and improve stability
- To display advertisements through Google AdMob
- To deliver local notifications, alarms, and planner reminders you configure
- To download and cache remote content for offline and online features
- To check for App updates through Google Play services

We do not use your information to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Permissions the App May Request

Depending on your device and how you use the App, Android may request or use the following permissions:

- Internet: to download calendar updates, images, audio, legal information, and other remote content
- Notifications (Android 13 and above): to show alarms, reminders, and planner notifications you enable
- Exact Alarms / Schedule Exact Alarm: to deliver alarms and reminders at the times you set
- Receive Boot Completed: to restore scheduled alarms and reminders after device restart
- Vibrate and Wake Lock: to support alarm and reminder behavior while ringing

You can control notification permission in your device settings. Some alarm features may not work correctly if exact alarm permission is denied on supported Android versions.

The App can work offline for many core calendar and reference features after content has been cached on your device.

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following situations:

Remote content cached on your device may be refreshed or replaced when the App checks for updates.

Because much of your personal App content is stored locally on your device, you should also protect your device with a screen lock and keep your operating system updated.
