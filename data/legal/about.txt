ABOUT THE APP

Last Updated: June 2026

Buddhist Calendar (also referred to as "Dhamma Darshan") is a Buddhist calendar and lifestyle reference application developed and maintained by RDroid Apps.

The App is designed to help users explore Buddhist observances, festivals, lunar events, historical references, sacred places, daily practice tools, and personal planning features in one place.

Main Features

Calendar and Events
- Buddhist calendar with daily events, festivals, and historical anniversaries
- Monthly calendar views with support for multiple calendar years where available
- Detailed date information including lunar and astronomical observances
- All Events list and date detail screens
- Offline access to much of the calendar and reference content after initial load

Reference and Learning
- Buddhist Places with descriptions and images
- Daily Quotes
- Buddhist History
- Five Precepts (Panchsheel)
- Chanting and Paritta texts with detail pages
- Chant audio and meditation sound playback, including optional import of audio from your device

Practice and Personal Tools
- Meditation timer with session history
- Daily Planner for tasks, notes, and optional reminders
- Alarms and Reminders for daily, weekly, and practice-related schedules
- Statistics for meditation and planner progress
- Tools and converters including Buddhist Era conversion
- Home screen widgets for date, holidays, moon phase, alarms, and related information

App Experience
- Modern Android interface with navigation drawer and related feature links
- Optional background music and sound effects (user configurable)
- User Guide and in-app legal information
- App update checks through Google Play when available

Online and Offline Use

Many core calendar and reference features work offline after content has been cached on your device. Some features require an internet connection, including content updates, certain images, server-hosted audio, advertisements, analytics, and online legal page loading.

Purpose

The App is intended for educational, cultural, devotional, and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for guidance from qualified teachers, monastic communities, or official religious authorities.

While every effort is made to provide useful and accurate information, users should independently verify important dates, observances, and historical details from trusted sources when required.
