Data Safety Information

Dhamma Darshan 2026 is designed to minimize data collection.

Information that may be collected automatically:

- Anonymous analytics data
- App interaction events
- Device performance information
- Advertising performance information

Data is used only for:

- App improvement
- Feature optimization
- Performance monitoring
- Advertisement management

The application does not provide user accounts and does not require users to submit personal information for normal operation.

Data is not sold to third parties.

Some information may be processed by trusted service providers including:

- Google Firebase Analytics
- Google AdMob

The Buddhist Places feature may access online resources to retrieve updated content.

Most calendar and event information remains available offline.
