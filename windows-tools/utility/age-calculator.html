About Age Calculator & Rashi Finder

This tool calculates your exact age and provides accurate zodiac sign (Rashi) based on Indian Vedic astrology. Unlike Western astrology which uses tropical zodiac, Indian astrology uses sidereal zodiac which accounts for the precession of equinoxes, giving more accurate results for Indian users.

Key Differences: Western vs Indian Zodiac

Western Zodiac (Tropical)

  • Based on seasons
  • Fixed to March equinox
  • Doesn't account for Earth's precession
  • Dates are fixed each year

Indian Zodiac (Sidereal)

  • Based on fixed stars
  • Accounts for Earth's precession
  • More accurate for astrology
  • Uses actual star positions

Example Difference

  • Sept 2, 1978, 02:15 AM, Mumbai
  • Western: Virgo (Kanya)
  • Indian: Leo (Sinh)
  • Difference: ~23-24 days shift

Why Birth Time & Place Matter

In Indian astrology, the exact birth time and place are crucial for:

  • Accurate Rashi: Moon sign calculation needs exact time
  • Nakshatra: Birth star depends on time and location
  • Lagna: Ascendant sign changes every 2 hours
  • Planetary Positions: Exact positions at birth moment

FAQ

Why is my Indian rashi different from Western zodiac? Western zodiac is based on seasons (tropical) while Indian zodiac is based on fixed stars (sidereal). There's about 23-24 days difference due to Earth's precession.

Which one is more accurate for astrology? For Indian/Vedic astrology, the sidereal (Indian) zodiac is more accurate as it uses actual star positions.

What if I don't know my exact birth time? Use approximate time. For rashi calculation, time within 1-2 hours is usually sufficient unless born near sign change.

Can I get my nakshatra (birth star) from this tool? This tool focuses on rashi. For nakshatra and detailed horoscope, consult an astrologer with exact birth details.
