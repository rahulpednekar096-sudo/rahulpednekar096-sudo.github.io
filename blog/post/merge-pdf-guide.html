Step 2: Select Your PDF Files

Three ways to add files:

Method 1: Drag & Drop

Drag PDF files from your computer directly into the browser window

Method 2: File Selector

Click "Choose Files" button to select PDFs from your device

Method 3: Multiple Selection

Advanced PDF Merge Techniques

1. Professional Document Organization

Scenario: Creating a business proposal with multiple sections

Optimal Order:

  1. Cover page (single page PDF)
  2. Executive summary
  3. Table of contents
  4. Main content sections
  5. Appendices and references
  6. Contact information page
Tip: Add page numbers after merging

2. Academic Paper Preparation

Scenario: Submitting research paper with multiple components

Workflow:

  • Merge: Abstract + Introduction
  • Add: Literature review
  • Insert: Methodology section
  • Include: Results and analysis
  • Append: Conclusion and references
  • Final: Declaration page
Check: Page numbering consistency

3. Photo Book Creation

Scenario: Creating digital photo album from individual photos

Process:

  1. Convert photos to PDF (one per page)
  2. Arrange in chronological order
  3. Add section divider pages
  4. Include captions as separate pages
  5. Merge all into single photo book
Tool: Use Image to PDF converter first
