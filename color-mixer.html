Explore, Create & Capture Colors

Color Mixer is a creative and powerful app that lets you mix, explore, and capture colors with ease. Whether you’re a designer, artist, or color enthusiast, discover how digital and traditional colors blend together to create endless shades. 🌈

✨ Key Features

  • 🎨 Color Mixing: Mix up to 8 colors using RYB (for artists) and RGB (for designers). Get instant HEX & RGB values.
  • 📚 Color Library: 900+ predefined shades organized by color families for learning and inspiration.
  • 📸 Color Capture: Pick colors from gallery or live camera with pixel-perfect magnifier.
  • 💾 Save & Manage: Save, rename, and reuse palettes anytime — all stored locally.
  • 📤 Share & Export: Export HEX/RGB codes or share color images for design tools like Figma, Photoshop, etc.

🎯 Who Is It For?

  • 🎨 Artists & Designers — Create perfect color palettes
  • 📚 Students — Learn color theory visually
  • 💻 Developers — Export HEX codes for UI design
  • 🏠 Interior Designers — Capture real-world shades
  • 🌈 Creative Enthusiasts — Discover infinite tones

🧠 Learn While You Create

Understand how RYB and RGB differ, explore complementary and triadic colors, and learn color harmony, balance, and saturation in real time — perfect for beginners and pros alike.

⚙️ Technical Highlights

  • ✅ Works fully offline
  • ⚡ Lightweight and battery-friendly
  • 🎨 Clean, modern interface
  • ↩️ Undo support for all actions
  • 📱 Supports Android 7.0+

🌐 Privacy & Performance

No login, no tracking, and no internet required. All your data is private and stored locally on your device. Fast, secure, and always ready when inspiration strikes.

💡 Tips

  • Try both RYB & RGB for tone differences
  • Use magnifier for pixel-perfect color picking
  • Save your best palettes for reuse
  • Blend neutral tones for realism
