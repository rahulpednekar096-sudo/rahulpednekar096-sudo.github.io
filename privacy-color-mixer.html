Privacy Policy – Color Mixer

Effective Date: 26 September 2025

🎨 About Color Mixer

Color Mixer is a creative tool that helps users mix colors through two professional blending systems:

  • Vivid / Natural (RYB): Painter-style color mixing
  • Classic / Digital (RGB): Display-based color blending
  • 8-slot system with adjustable quantities
  • 90+ color variations per base color
  • Favorites and palette management features

1. Data Collection

Stored locally (on your device only):

  • Color recipes, palettes, favorites
  • Mixing method & UI preferences

Collected by third-party services (ads or analytics):

If the app uses third-party advertising or analytics services, those services may automatically collect limited, non-personal device information such as:

  • Advertising ID (anonymous identifier)
  • Approximate location (region-level)
  • Device type, model, and OS version
  • App usage and performance data

We use the Android Advertising ID to serve ads and analyze app usage. This identifier is used only for advertising and analytics purposes. It is not linked to personally identifiable information such as your name or email.

We do not link your Advertising ID with your Google account information (such as name or email). These data sources are handled separately and are not combined for profiling.

We do not directly collect or store personal information such as name or email on our own servers. However, if you choose to sign in using Google, such information is securely processed by Firebase Authentication (Google services).

The app may use Firebase Analytics (a Google service) to collect anonymous usage data such as app interactions, screen views, and feature usage. This data does not include personally identifiable information and is used only to improve app performance and user experience.

2. User Login (Google Sign-In)

If you choose to sign in using Google, the app uses Firebase Authentication (a Google service) to securely authenticate your account.

During this process, the following information may be accessed:

  • Your name (display name)
  • Your email address

This information is used only to personalize your experience within the app (e.g., greeting and avatar display).

We do not store, share, or sell this personal information on our own servers.

Authentication is handled securely by Google Firebase services.

Google services are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy .

3. Purpose of Use

  • Provide and improve color-mixing features
  • Remember user settings and preferences
  • We may show personalized or non-personalized advertisements using third-party services such as Google AdMob. Users can control ad personalization through their device settings.
  • Analyze performance and improve stability

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personally identifiable information.

Non-personal data may be shared only with advertising or analytics services for delivering, measuring, or improving app performance.

5. Your Choices & Control

  • Switch between RYB and RGB modes anytime in-app
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Clear all local data anytime: Settings → Apps → Color Mixer → Storage → Clear Data

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us to remove it.

7. Security

Most data in Color Mixer is stored locally on your device. When using features such as Google Sign-In, data is securely processed through Google Firebase services over encrypted HTTPS connections.

8. Data Deletion Request
