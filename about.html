About RDroid Apps

RDroid Apps is an independent software project focused on building simple, useful, and privacy-respecting applications across multiple platforms. The project is primarily developed and maintained by a developer, with ideas, feedback, and suggestions coming from friends, collaborators, and modern development tools.

Our Cross-Platform Vision

The goal of RDroid Apps is to create practical software that works reliably across devices and platforms. What started with Android applications has naturally expanded to include Windows tools that complement mobile workflows. Whether on a phone or a desktop, RDroid Apps aims to provide clear, focused, and easy-to-use solutions for everyday digital tasks.
