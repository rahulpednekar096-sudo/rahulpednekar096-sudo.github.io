🎵 Pocket Music Composer - User Guide

Welcome to Pocket Music Composer by RDroid Apps!

Here's how to use the app:

1️⃣ Start a New Track

  • Tap on "New Track" to begin.
  • Add musical notes like C4, D#3, A#4 etc.
  • Adjust each note's duration and position using the sliders.

2️⃣ Play Your Track

  • Tap the "Play" button to listen to your composition.
  • Use Pause, Stop, or Replay controls anytime.
  • Play, pause, stop, or replay your composition at any time.

3️⃣ Save and Load

  • Save your current track to local storage for later editing.
  • Load any previously saved track from your device storage.
  • Organize tracks with custom names and timestamps.

4️⃣ Share Your Music

  • Export your track as audio file (M4A/WAV format).
  • Share directly to social media or messaging apps.
  • Send your composition to friends via email or Bluetooth.

5️⃣ Track View & Notes

  • Each musical note is displayed clearly on the timeline.
  • Use the available controls to adjust note order and timing.
  • Visual layout helps understand timing and flow of composition.
  • Color-coded notes for easy identification of pitch and duration.

💡 Pro Tips for Better Compositions

  • Begin with simple melodies made from individual notes before building longer patterns.
  • Use the metronome feature to maintain rhythm consistency.
  • Experiment with different note durations for varied expression.
  • Layer multiple tracks for richer, fuller sound.
  • Save multiple versions to compare and choose the best one.
